Aviation Parts Marketplace
Front-End Training Project

Screens:
1. Main Screen
- Products List (Each Item Card includes: small image, name, PN, price, and "add to cart" button);
- List of items in Cart (in a form of small image-buttons);
- "Go-to-cart" button;
- List of previous orders.

2. Product Card Screen
Detailed Product (Item) Card includes:
- Item image;
- Item name;
- Item PN;
- Item Price;
- Item Condition;
- Item Certification;
- Item Lead Time;
- "Add to cart" button.

3. Cart Screen
- List of items in cart. Each Item Card includes:
    - - Item image;
    - - Item name;
    - - Item PN;
    - - Item Condition;
    - - Item Certification;
    - - Item Lead Time;
    - - Item Price;
    - - "Add/Remove Qty" buttons.
- Total Price Information.
- "Checkout" button.

4. Cart Checkout Screen
- Bill-to-address and "Update" button.
- Payment Method information and "Update" button.
- List of items in cart.
- Order Price Summary (items, handling, total).
- "Place order" button.

5. Order Details Screen
- Order number.
- Bill-to-address and "Update" button.
- Payment Method information and "Update" button.
- List of items in cart.
- Order Price Summary (items, handling, total).

Technology to use:
html, css, javascript, Parcel, Firestore

Hosting:
GitHub Pages

Back-End / DB:
Google Firebase
